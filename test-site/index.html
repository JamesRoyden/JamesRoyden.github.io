Coca-Cola

Coca-cola is a drinks company that was founded in 2019, its products consist of:

  • Coca-Cola
  • Coca-Cola Zero
  • Diet Coca-Cola
  • Coca-Cola Life

They have tried to diversify their product range over the years to try and attract customers who enjoy Coca-Cola but are looking for a healthier alternative as a result they have produced products that contain reduced amount of sugar, low fat and low calorie options
